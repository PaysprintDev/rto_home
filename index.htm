Unlock Homeownership Sooner with a Co-Owning Partner

Achieve your dream of owning a home without the stress of additional debt

Your Path to Homeownership Starts Here

Owning a home is a significant milestone, but saving for a 20% down payment can be challenging. We’re here to make it possible. As your co-owning partner, we contribute 5% to 15% of the home’s value, helping you reach that 20% down payment faster and start your journey to homeownership sooner. This isn't a loan, so there’s no interest or additional debt—just a shared investment in your future. To get started, simply complete the evaluation form and we take it from there.

Simplifying the Homebuying Process

We invest alongside you to make homeownership a reality. Here’s how it works:

Co-Investment

We contribute a portion of the down payment, reducing your mortgage burden.

Shared Equity

Our investment earns us a share in the home’s future value, aligning our interests with yours.

Flexible Options

You’re free to sell the home or buy out our share whenever it suits you.

Imagine purchasing a $1M home. With an 8% contribution from you and a 12% contribution from us, we achieve a 20% down payment together. You own 40% of the equity, and we own 60%. When the home appreciates, we share in the gains.
